## Decisions Made

- **app:app target in CMD:** The uvicorn entrypoint must target the `socketio.ASGIApp` wrapper (`app`) not the raw FastAPI instance (`fastapi_app`). Pointing at `fastapi_app` would cause all SocketIO upgrade requests to receive 404s. This is documented in STATE.md pre-v2 decisions.
- **--workers 1:** python-socketio maintains per-session state in memory; multiple workers would break session routing.
- **python-socketio asyncio extra warning:** uv warns that `python-socketio==5.14.3` has no `asyncio` extra. This is benign — async support is bundled in 5.x by default, not gated behind an extra. Imports succeed.
