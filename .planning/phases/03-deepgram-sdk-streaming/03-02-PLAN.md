  <what-built>
    Full Deepgram SDK streaming via AsyncDeepgramClient.listen.v1.connect():
    - Per-session asyncio.Task with _sessions dict
    - Keep-alive every 8 seconds (STR-03)
    - Graceful stop: send_close_stream() + await listen_task before stream_finished (STR-04)
    - No threading or websocket-client (STR-01, STR-02)
  </what-built>
  <how-to-verify>
    Prerequisites: DEEPGRAM_API_KEY must be set in .env or shell environment.

    1. Open http://localhost:8001 in a browser with a microphone.

    2. STR-01 + STR-02 — Live transcription works:
       - Click Start. Confirm "stream_started" appears in browser console/UI.
       - Speak a sentence (e.g., "Testing one two three").
       - Confirm transcription_update events appear with transcript text and is_final fields.
       - Expected: interim (is_final=false) results appear while speaking, then a final result (is_final=true).

    3. STR-03 — Keep-alive during pause:
       - Start transcription.
       - Stay silent for 15+ seconds.
       - Confirm the SocketIO connection remains open (no stream_finished event fires).
       - Then speak again — transcription should resume immediately.

    4. STR-04 — No dropped final words:
       - Say a sentence ending with a distinct final word (e.g., "The last word is apple").
       - Click Stop immediately after the last word.
       - Confirm "apple" appears in the transcript before stream_finished fires.
       - Confirm stream_finished fires after all transcript text is received.

    5. STR-02 — Start/stop/start cycle:
       - Stop transcription. Wait for stream_finished.
       - Start again. Confirm stream_started fires again.
       - Speak — transcription should work on the second session.
       - No doubled transcription_update events (from an orphaned task).
  </how-to-verify>
